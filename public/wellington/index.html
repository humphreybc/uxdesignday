  • Level up.

UX Design Day Wellington is a one-day, action packed event! Come with no knowledge of User Experience (UX) or design thinking and leave with an understanding which will rock your business world.

This is not just a conference for graphic, web, software or UX designers. If you are in the business of creating products and services, then this event will help you to understand how to better resonate with your customers and keep up with the latest innovations and practices. With our amazing local speakers, you will discover innovations happening right here. As well as our talented locals, Samsung USA, have let us borrow their Creative Director, Wesley Yun to share his knowledge!

Leave with true insights, real practical knowledge and a new network of great people. Get ready for a day of inspiring, funny and ‘light bulb moments’ from some of Wellington’s’ finest.
