UX Design Day 2014 is a one-day conference being held at the Dunedin Public Art Gallery on Friday, 31st October, 2014. It’s for anyone with an interest in digital design and User Experience.

This is not just a conference for designers. With a wide range of talks encompassing all facets of UX design and from different points of view, we hope to highlight the importance of good design, help you introduce design practices & principles into your current workflow, and inspire you to learn new skills.

Be prepared for an action-packed day with engaging, inspirational talks in the morning followed by a practical hands-on workshop in the afternoon led by experts.

We’ll feed and water you, just turn up and be ready to learn!
