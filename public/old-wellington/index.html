UX Design Day Wellington is a one-day, action packed event! Come with no knowledge of User Experience (UX) or design thinking and leave with an understanding which will rock your business world.

UX is at the heart of how people engage with, use, and fall in love with your product, service and brand - both in the digital and real worlds.

UX Design Day Wellington is an all-inclusive, one-day conference that will help you work smarter to make your solutions customer-centric and more likely to succeed.

This is not just a conference for graphic, web, software or UX designers. If you are in the business of creating products and services, then this event will help you to understand how to better resonate with your customers and keep up with the latest innovations and practices.

With our amazing local speakers, you will discover innovations happening right here. As well as our talented locals, global mobile and consumer electronics giant Samsung have let us borrow their Creative Director, Wesley Yun, all the way from San Francisco. Formerly Head of UX at Motorola and Palm, Wesley will be speaking at the next SXSW, but we get him first!

Leave with true insights, real practical knowledge and a new network of great people. Get ready for a day of inspiring, funny and ‘light bulb moments’ from some of Wellington’s’ finest.
